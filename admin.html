Apps Script Web App URL

After you create and deploy your Apps Script Web App for handling the Apply form, paste its URL here for reference and also set it as the action in the form on index.html.

Example format (replace with your real one):
https://script.google.com/macros/s/YOUR_SCRIPT_ID/exec
Basic flow:
  • Sellers fill out the Apply form on index.html.
  • The form POSTs to your Apps Script Web App URL.
  • Apps Script appends a new row to your Wholesale eBay Products – Applications Google Sheet at:
    https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1EmyVj4waoPIqeK-EgrUmPtJklsIV7ePGHR7PmU_uQU4/edit?gid=0#gid=0
  • You review the sheet and email approved sellers from watchzapnsave@gmail.com with their product package and personal Google Sheet.
