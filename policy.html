Privacy & Return Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2025

This Privacy & Return Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how returns, refunds, and replacements are handled for both digital and physical products.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you voluntarily submit, including:

  • Name
  • Email addresses
  • WhatsApp/phone contact
  • eBay usernames
  • Order data entered in your personal Google Sheet

We do not collect payment card numbers; all payments are processed securely through your chosen payment method.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used for:

  • Supplying product packages
  • Processing wholesale orders
  • Shipping and fulfillment
  • Providing customer support
  • Preventing fraud and enforcing marketplace compliance

We do not sell or rent your information to any third party.

3. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored on secure systems, including Google Sheets and email servers. Access is restricted to authorized personnel only.

While we use industry-standard safeguards, no online platform can guarantee 100% security.

4. Return Policy

4.1 Physical Products

Physical products may be returned only if they are:

  • Unopened
  • Unused
  • In original condition and packaging
  • Within the normal eBay return window (usually 30 days)

Once we receive the return and verify its condition, we will issue a refund of the wholesale price.

4.2 Digital Products

Digital downloads, software licenses, and product keys are not refundable.

If a key does not activate or is defective, we will replace it with a new working key free of charge.

4.3 Wrong or Damaged Items

If you receive an incorrect or damaged item due to our error, we will:

  • Cover return shipping (if needed)
  • Ship a corrected replacement

4.4 Buyer’s Remorse or Incorrect Listings

Returns due to incorrect listings, inaccurate descriptions, or seller-created errors are the responsibility of the seller. We are not liable for customer dissatisfaction caused by misrepresentation or wrong information entered by the seller.

5. Shipping Policy

All products are shipped directly from our U.S. warehouse. Tracking is provided for all shipments.

We are not responsible for:

  • Delays caused by USPS, UPS, FedEx, etc.
  • Lost packages after confirmed delivery
  • Incorrect addresses provided by sellers
